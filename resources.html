Educational Resources

Community & Support Resources for Families

Navigating autism support can feel overwhelming. Below you'll find Facebook communities, local Lanark County resources, and province-wide organizations to help you and your family feel connected and informed.

Please note: Group membership, activity levels, and details may change over time. We recommend clicking through to verify each group or organization is currently active before joining. Continuum ABA is not affiliated with any of the groups listed below.

Lanark County & Eastern Ontario

Local services and directories serving the Lanark region

eMentalHealth — Lanark County Directory

A curated directory of autism and ASD services, support groups, and mental health resources specifically for Lanark County, including Perth, Smiths Falls, and Carleton Place.

View directory

Kids Inclusive — OAP Services

The Ontario Autism Program (OAP) service provider for Lanark, Leeds & Grenville, Frontenac, Hastings, and surrounding counties. First point of contact for OAP-funded services.

kidsinclusive.ca 613-544-3400 ext. 23175

Champlain Healthline — Autism Directory

Regional health directory covering the Champlain area (Ottawa Valley and surrounding region), listing autism support groups, organizations, and services near Lanark County.

Search the directory

South East Healthline — Autism Services

Covers Lanark, Leeds & Grenville and surrounding South East Ontario counties. Lists local autism support groups, advocacy organizations, and family programs.

Search the directory

Facebook Support Groups

Online communities open to Ontario families — join from anywhere

Autism Parents Support Group for Families in Ontario

Province-wide

A private Facebook group for parents and caregivers of children with autism across Ontario. Members share experiences, resources, tips, and emotional support. Open to all Ontario families.

View on Facebook

Autism Support Group for Parents in Ontario

Province-wide

A Facebook community dedicated to connecting Ontario parents and caregivers raising autistic children. A space to ask questions, share strategies, and find solidarity with families who understand.

View on Facebook

Ontario Autism Coalition

Advocacy

A parent-led advocacy group pushing for improved autism services and funding across Ontario. Stay informed on OAP policy changes, funding announcements, and how to advocate for the services they need.

Facebook Group Official Page

Autism Parent Support Group — Ottawa & Area

Closest to Lanark

Based in Ottawa and serving Eastern Ontario — the closest major regional Facebook group to Lanark County. Families from Perth, Smiths Falls, and Carleton Place are welcome. Active discussion of local Ottawa Valley resources.

View on Facebook

Provincial Organizations

Established organizations offering support, advocacy, and programs across Ontario

Autism Ontario

Ontario's largest autism organization. Offers chapter-based parent support groups, family events, the CARES caregiver program, and advocacy. Free to access — no diagnosis required to join most programs.

autismontario.com

Autism Canada

National organization providing education, resources, and advocacy for autistic individuals and their families. Offers research-informed articles and a resource library for parents.

autismcanada.org

Kinark Autism Services

Free foundational family services for families registered in the Ontario Autism Program (OAP), including caregiver coaching, family consultation, and transition support.

kinarkautismservices.ca

End The Wait Ontario

A parent-built directory of free autism support groups across Ontario. Searchable by region — useful for finding groups closest to Lanark County that aren't easily found through Google.

Browse the directory

Ontario Autism Program (OAP)

The Government of Ontario's core autism funding program. Learn about eligibility, how to register, funding amounts, and urgent response services available to your family.

ontario.ca — OAP
